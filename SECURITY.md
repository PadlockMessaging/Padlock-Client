## Reporting a Vulnerability

Reporting vulnerabilities privately is extremely important for everyone.

Public disclosure before a fix is available gives adversaries a clear path to exploit our users, particularly given the open source nature of the codebase.

You can securely report a vulnerability on the **Security and quality page.**

<img width="1920" height="48" alt="photo" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/93f1facf-ec9e-42eb-950e-e75ef3a5a053" />

When reporting, please include steps to reproduce, affected version, and potential impact. Reports affecting the encryption layer, authentication, or key management will be treated as critical priority.

We will acknowledge all vulnerability reports within **72 hours**. We aim to resolve critical vulnerabilities within **14 days** and will keep the reporter informed throughout the process. We ask reporters to allow us reasonable time to issue a fix before any public disclosure.
